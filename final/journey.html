Our Journey

At Pacific Trails Resort, our story begins with a love for nature and the desire to create a peaceful place where guests can reconnect with themselves and the world around them. Surrounded by the beauty of California’s coastline, our resort combines comfort and adventure in perfect harmony. From the gentle sound of the waves to the quiet trails that wind through the forest, every detail is designed to offer balance and inspiration. Our team believes in sustainable hospitality, warm service, and unforgettable experiences. Whether you come for hiking, kayaking, or simply to rest under the stars, Pacific Trails invites you to slow down, breathe deeply, and rediscover the simple joy of being close to nature.

Our Values

Our values are simple: stewardship, community, and well-being. We protect the coast through low-impact operations, support local makers and guides, and create spaces that restore body and mind. We lead with warm, thoughtful service, fairness, and safety. Every choice—from ingredients to materials—reflects respect for nature, people, and the memories guests take home for years to come.
